About the BUBT Programming Contest

The Bangladesh University of Business and Technology (BUBT) is thrilled to present the BUBT Inter-University Collaborative Programming Contest 2025!

As the proud host of the ICPC Asia Dhaka Regional Contest, BUBT is elevating its tradition of intra-university programming contests by transforming it into an inter-university collaborative event that unites passionate programmers from across the nation. This contest offers an incredible opportunity for students to experience the thrill of real competitive programming, sharpen their problem-solving and teamwork skills, and build connections with fellow coders from other universities.

For more information on Dhaka Regional Contest, visit the Dhaka Regional Contest site.
